Rent To Own

Welcome to our rent-to-own program, where you can rent a high-quality violin for a set period of time with the option to purchase it at the end of the lease term. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, our program is designed to make it easier for you to get started or upgrade your current

Here's how it works:

Choose your instrument: We offer a variety of violins in different sizes and levels, from beginner to professional-grade. You can choose the instrument that best fits your needs and budget.

Rent the instrument: You can rent the violin for a set period of time, typically ranging from three to twelve months. During the rental period, you'll make monthly payments that include the rental fee and a portion of the purchase price.

Apply rental credit towards purchase: If you decide to purchase the instrument at the end of the lease term, a portion of the rental payments you made will be applied towards the purchase price. This makes it easier to own the instrument without having to pay for it all at once.

Flexible options: If you decide not to purchase the instrument, you can return it at the end of the lease term with no further obligation. Alternatively, you can extend the rental period or upgrade to a different instrument.

Our rent-to-own program is designed to be flexible and affordable, so you can focus on playing and enjoying your instrument. We also offer additional services such as instrument maintenance, repair, and rental insurance to ensure that you get the most out of your rental experience.

If you're interested in our rent-to-own program or have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're happy to help you find the perfect violin and make your musical journey a success.
