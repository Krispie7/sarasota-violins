Rent To Own

Welcome to our Rent to Own program. Whether you are a beginner or a more advanced player, our Rent to Own program is designed to make it easy and affordable for you to get started in playing a string instrument or to upgrade your current instrument.

Here's how it works:

  • Choose your instrument: We offer a variety of instruments in different sizes and levels, from beginner to more advanced. You can choose the instrument that best fits your needs and budget. The monthly rental rate is set according to the retail price of the instrument. Rentals start as low as $20 per month.

    • Our Rent-to-Own program is designed to be flexible and affordable so you can focus on playing.

  • If you wish to return the instrument: Unless there is damage, your deposit will be refunded when you return the instrument.

  • If you wish to keep the instrument: There will be no more payments after the completion of the contract (typically 2 to 3 years). After that, the instrument is yours to keep. Congratulations!
